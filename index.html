An Apple a Day

by: Amukta Nayak (apnayak)

Multiplayer snake: grow and eat as many apples as you can.

Compete to eat as many apples as you can. Block and starve out your opponent. Winner is whoever stays alive the longest.

Gameplay

Like snake, the game begins with a top-down view of a grid. Each player controls a snake (WASD/LRUD to move). The board is populated with 4-5 apples placed randomly at all times. When a snake eats an apple, it grows by one unit.

When a snake's head collides into another snake, the first snake dies. Strategically block your opponents from apples.

The winner is the snake that survives the longest.
